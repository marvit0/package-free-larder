The Package Free Larder

Freeing Portsmouth from Plastic.

Join us now!
Portsmouth finds itself in the midst of exciting times.
The tide is turning and we are no longer willing to accept unnecessary packaging and single use plastic as an inevitable fact of shopping for our groceries.
It’s time for a change!

We have three goals

Create a zero waste supermarket

free of packaging where people can buy dry goods in bulk, cleaning products and toiletries and fresh produce box collection scheme from local suppliers

Champion and support

the ending of household waste going to landfill and incinerators in Portsmouth by 2025

Change attitudes and reliance

on single use plastic everywhere, in recognition of the consequences on our beautiful planet and responsibility we all have to care for it

So what next?

To begin…. we are looking for people to join our team. Our vision is to create a community led packaging free supermarket that is run by the community for the community.
We are already 400 people strong and we are looking to increase our number to 1000 by September 2018.
